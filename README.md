An interactive New Year's Eve celebration card featuring a Times Square-style ball drop, fireworks, and falling snow.

## Features

- **Countdown Timer**: 10-second countdown with animated ball drop
- **Times Square Building**: Iconic NYC skyline with lit windows and snow-capped rooftops
- **Fireworks Display**: Colorful fireworks launch after the countdown
- **Falling Snow**: Animated snowflakes throughout the scene
- **Romantic Couple**: Silhouette of a couple on a rooftop with twinkling stars
- **Mobile Responsive**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

1. Clone or download this repository
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Watch the countdown and enjoy the celebration!

## Features Breakdown

### Ball Drop Animation
- Ball starts at the top of the Times Square tower
- Drops over 10 seconds alongside the countdown
- Stays behind the billboard for realistic depth

### Billboard Display
- Shows countdown numbers (10-0)
- Transitions to "Happy New Year 2026!" message
- Glowing border and text effects

### Fireworks
- Particle-based animation using HTML5 Canvas
- Random colors and positions
- Launches continuously after countdown reaches zero

### Snow Effects
- Falling snowflakes with varying sizes and speeds
- Snow caps on building rooftops
- Creates winter atmosphere
